About

I am a Full-Stack Web Developer for small businesses to help them improve and grow. My purpose is to help small businesses grow in success and size. I want to further technology in the web development field to make it as easy as importing a couple of pictures to create a full website by the year 2050.

Case Studies

  • Dino Dad

    Dino Dad's Journey: A Dinosaur Adventure is an up and coming book series centered around the Dino family. These are children's books with great illustrations to help engage the children. Dino Dad's Journey was in need of a website in order to increase engagement and show off their books.

  • For A Loop

    "For A Loop" is a small woman-owned business that is run by one person. This person creates every object by hand. She crotchets and knits every item with love and puts in a lot of effort. It started as a hobby and has turned into a small business. They wanted a website to showcase their products similar to a portfolio, and they wanted the website to give a good way for customers to contact the business owner.

  • One Heck of a Resort

    One Heck of a Resort is a resort that operates year round in the Swiss Alps. They have plenty of amenities for every time of year with beautiful scenery. It is the perfect escape to get away from your busy life and is loved by the local village. One Heck of a Resort required a website that showcases small bits of their resort and provides a contact form and currently have no website.
